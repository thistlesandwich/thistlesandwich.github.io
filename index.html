Welcome

I am a personal organiser based in London and Surrey in the UK.

What I can help you with

Responsibly dispose of items you don't need, organise the items you keep, and learn how to simplify and maintain your space. My services are not just limited to physical possessions; see below for some examples. Feel free to email me if you have any questions or would like to discuss a potential project.

A single room Wardrobe, kitchen cupboards, living room, garage...

The whole house General declutter, helping out a relative...

Your computer and online data Documents and photos, emails, subscriptions...

Paperwork Finance and admin, school/uni/business documents...

Other advice Event planning, calendars, schedules, budgeting...

What to expect

I don't use a specific method but instead what I have learnt through experience; I take aspects from lots of different ideas to personalise my service for each client. See my info page for inspiration.

Together we will find storage solutions using your existing possessions as much as possible, and find a method that works for you. I will teach you how to use different resources for disposal, recycling, reusing, selling, and how to stay organised.

An initial consultation is free of charge. £30/hour thereafter plus materials.
