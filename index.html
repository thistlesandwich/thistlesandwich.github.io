Welcome!

We are an energetic female-fronted rock band in the UK: three girls upfront with Helen on guitar, Nicole on bass, Maxine on vocals... and Guy on drums! We bring you upbeat classic rock covers you love from across the decades, that maybe you haven’t heard for a while, as well as adding other genres in our own style to mix things up and keep you dancing. We are based around Hertfordshire, but are happy to travel anywhere to rock out and party!

Check out our social media and links for contact details, bookings and finding out what we're up to.

We are excited to see you soon!
